About Toted

Sacramento Built. Move Ready.

Toted is a Sacramento-based moving tote rental service built for apartment renters who are tired of scrambling for boxes, taping them together, and hauling flat cardboard to the recycling bin at midnight.

FAQ

Common Questions.

How do I pay? +
After you submit your booking request, we'll confirm availability and send you a payment link via email. We accept all major cards. Payment is due before delivery.
What if I need the totes longer than expected? +
No stress. Just email us before your rental period ends and we'll add a week for $25. Late returns without notice may incur a $10/day fee.
What size are the totes? +
Our standard totes are 27-gallon heavy-duty industrial bins with locking lids. About 30" x 20" x 15" — big enough for clothes, books, kitchen items, and most household goods.
Do you help with packing or moving? +
We're a tote rental service — we deliver and pick up, but we don't pack or move your stuff. Need movers? We can recommend a few good local companies.
What if a tote gets damaged? +
Normal wear and tear is fine — these totes are built tough. Intentional damage or loss is billed at $15 per tote. We'll note any issues at pickup and follow up by email.
Can I get same-day or next-day delivery? +
We do our best! Email us at hello@totedsac.com and we'll let you know what's available. Rush bookings may have a small convenience fee depending on timing.
